## Design Specifications Summary

### Style 1: Academic/Professional
- **Best for:** Academic presentations, formal capstone defenses, research conferences
- **Strengths:** Credible, readable, data-focused, professional
- **Typography:** Serif titles, sans-serif body
- **Colors:** Navy, teal, gray, white
- **Visuals:** Clean charts, traditional layouts

### Style 2: Tech/Modern
- **Best for:** Tech conferences, developer audiences, portfolio showcases
- **Strengths:** Eye-catching, modern, tech-savvy aesthetic
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif, monospace accents
- **Colors:** Dark background, neon accents (cyan, purple, green)
- **Visuals:** Glowing effects, terminal-inspired, vibrant charts

### Style 3: Data-Driven/Infographic
- **Best for:** General audiences, stakeholder presentations, social media sharing
- **Strengths:** Accessible, engaging, visual storytelling
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif, playful yet professional
- **Colors:** Vibrant multi-color palette (orange, blue, yellow, purple)
- **Visuals:** Icons, badges, infographic elements, simplified charts
